The Reality

What's happening in your market right now

Regional newsletters, hyperlocal blogs, and digital ad networks are approaching your advertisers with packages that include email newsletters, web display, and social media. They're positioning themselves as the "digital complement" to print — a role that should be yours.

The publishers who thrive in the next five years won't be the ones who ignored digital. They'll be the ones who offered their advertisers a multi-platform package — print plus digital — under one relationship. INBXIFY makes that possible without building a digital team.

Honest Answers

We hear these concerns from every publisher

"I don't have a digital team."
You don't need one. INBXIFY is the team. We handle templates, delivery, CMS, analytics, and ad product infrastructure. Your staff focuses on content and sales — the things they're already good at.
"I can't afford to lose my advertisers to a platform."
INBXIFY doesn't sell ads. You do. We provide infrastructure, not competition. Your advertiser relationships stay yours. The newsletter becomes another product in your portfolio, not a replacement for your existing ones.
"I don't have an email list."
We provide immediate access to verified hyperlocal subscribers in your market. No building from zero, no minimum thresholds. Your first issue goes to real, engaged readers.
"Digital newsletters are a commodity now."
Generic newsletters are. Yours won't be. INBXIFY newsletters carry your editorial voice, your local brand, your advertiser relationships. The infrastructure is turnkey — the content and relationships are uniquely yours.
"We tried email before and it didn't work."
Most publishers try email with MailChimp templates, no ad products, and no subscriber base. That's not a newsletter business — it's a side project. INBXIFY is full infrastructure with multiple monetizable ad products from day one.
