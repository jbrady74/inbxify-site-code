About INBXIFY

We believe local publishers should own the digital relationship with their advertisers

INBXIFY exists because of a simple observation: local print publishers are losing their most valuable asset — their advertiser relationships — to digital-first competitors who offer newsletter packages the publishers can't match on their own.

The problem isn't that publishers lack ambition or business sense. It's that standing up a professional digital newsletter operation requires infrastructure, technology, and subscriber acquisition capabilities that most local publishers don't have — and shouldn't have to build from scratch.

INBXIFY is the infrastructure layer. We provide the technology, the verified subscriber base, the ad product suite, and the operational backbone so that publishers can focus on what they've always been best at: creating local content and maintaining advertiser relationships.

We're a small, focused company built by people who understand both publishing and technology. We're not trying to replace publishers or compete with them. We're building the tools that let them compete in a digital market — on their terms, under their brand, with their relationships intact.
