What to look for

Subject vs. From
Subject line emoji are far more common than From name emoji. They draw the eye to the second line — useful when your sender name is unfamiliar but your subject is the hook.
Crowding effect
Look at how many subjects already have emoji (🚨 🔥 🍂 💡 💰). Your 🏡 might blend in rather than stand out. Counter-strategy: clean From + clean subject = the calm one.
Variant D — 📍
The location pin is interesting for hyperlocal. It says "this is about a place" before the reader even reads. Less common than 🏡 in this category.
Recommended test
Pick the cleanest variant (A) plus your best-performing emoji variant (B or D). Run a 2-way A/B over 4 weekly sends before deciding. Don't overcomplicate the test.
