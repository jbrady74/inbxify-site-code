Engagement Model

The commitment structure

12–16 weeks

INBXIFY builds your complete newsletter infrastructure during this phase. Templates are designed, subscriber lists are provisioned, ad products are configured, and your web presence goes live. You focus on content pipeline and advertiser conversations. Reduced rate during build-out.

Phase 2

Standard Commitment

6 months

Full-service newsletter operations. Weekly (or bi-weekly) issues delivered to your subscribers with your content and your advertisers' placements. Analytics, ad tracking, subscriber management, and infrastructure maintenance all included. You sell ads and create content.
