Advertising Products

A full advertising product suite your sales team can sell from day one

INBXIFY newsletters aren't just emails — they're a full advertising platform. Feature articles, sponsor placements, display ads, directory listings, event promotions, and more. Every product is designed for hyperlocal relevance and measurable performance.

Feature Content

Feature Article

Full-length editorial content with dedicated hero image, byline, and interior photography. Premium top-of-newsletter placement.

Themed Spotlight

Category-grouped editorial showcasing businesses within a curated theme. High engagement through topical relevance.

Greeting / Intro

Publisher's editorial voice opening each issue. Sets tone and drives readership of sponsored content below.

Display Advertising

Banner Ad

High-visibility display placement within newsletter body. Standard 600×200 creative with click-through tracking.

FA Sponsor Ad

Sponsor badge and creative paired directly with a Feature Article. Association with premium editorial content.

TS Sponsor Ad

Sponsor placement within Themed Spotlight sections. Contextual relevance drives higher click-through rates.

Discovery & Listings

The Find / LBP

Local Business Profile cards within the discovery section. Business details, photos, and direct links in a browsable format.

Event Listing

Community event promotion with date, venue, description, and registration links. High local engagement.

RE Listing

Real estate property cards with photos, pricing, features, and agent links. Targeted to hyperlocal home buyers.

Directory & Digital

Featured Directory Listing

Premium placement in the searchable online business directory. Enhanced profile with photos, categories, and contact info.

Rotating Banner

Round-robin display ads across web pages. Automated scheduling with pause windows and issue-based controls.

Newsletter Archive Ads

Persistent ad placements within the searchable newsletter archive. Long-tail visibility for past-issue readers.
