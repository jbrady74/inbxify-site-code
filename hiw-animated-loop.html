THE RECURRING LOOP
1
You publish your print title
As you always do. Nothing changes about how you make it.
2
We build newsletters from your content
Repurposing your stories into a publication plan of weekly issues.
3
We send to your circulation area
Using our email database, matched to the community your title already serves.
4
You sell the new ad space
Fresh inventory you sell to your own advertisers.
5
You earn new revenue
Net-new income, with no new workload.
